    • About Us

    SH'VAT Shipping & Logistics LLC was incorporated in the years 2022 in U.A.E to cater to the ever increasing demand for freight forwarding and shipping services - Air, Sea & Land transport. To start with, the core activity was customs broker within UAE, however we realized the need of the hour was not limited to customs broker and we took on the onus of developing the export/import of Air, Sea & Land freight as well as from/to various origins/destinations. Our main focus is to be one of the leading service providers in the market for freight forwarding within GCC and across Globe bringing transparency to both customers and suppliers in the transportation industry. We aim to shed light on the traditional transportation market and make it transparent, easy, and cost-effective. Also, we provide the ultimate customer experience that brings efficiency to different layers of your supply chain management.

    OUR MISSION

    At Sh’vat , we dedicate our time to improving the transportation industry. We use cutting-edge technologies, state-of-the-art equipment, and a team full of professionals to provide you with the smartest logistics solutions.

    OUR VISION

    Our team is developing the most advanced platform that you AI-enabled operations assistant handles all your supply chain, shipments, cargo, and freight.
